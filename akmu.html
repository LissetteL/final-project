AKMU

Akdong Musician (악동뮤지션), also known as AKMU, consists of 2 members (brother and sister): Chanhyuk and Suhyun. They debuted under YG Entertainment on April 6, 2014.

Achievements

  • They won Kpop Star season 2.

  • The songs from their debut album, “Play” (11 songs all composed by Chanhyuk) have a total of more than 6 mil. downloads.

  • Chanhyuk is the composer of all AKMU’s songs and he plays the guitar.

  • 2012, Rookie Of The Month (December) "You are Attractive" (매력있어)

  • 2014, the silblings won Top 10 Artist.

  • In 2015 their song 200% won Gaon Chart Music Awards Song of the Year (April).

  • In 2015 the Korean Advertising Congress awarded AKMU the Grand Prize for Advertisement Model.

  • Suhyun starred in the 2017 k-drama “Part-Time Idol”.

  • In 2017, "Last Goodbye" won Best Rock/Folk Song, Top 5 Most Downloaded Songs, and Top 5 Most Searched Songs.

  • Suhyun appeared in the show ‘Part Time Idol’ along with JBJ’s Kwon Hyunbin.

  • 2020, Korea First Brand Awards they won the Singer-songwriter Award.
