Jump to Achievements


Ben is a South Korean singer under MAJOR9.

She first debuted in 2010 as the main vocalist of the group BeBe Mignon, under YWHO Enterprise.

She made her solo debut on September 18, 2012 with the single "Don't Go Today".





Ben Facts


– She can play the piano and the drums.
– Before signing to Major9 (formerly The Vibe Label), Ben was under Happyface Entertainment.
- She is know as the OST QUEEN.
- She has collborated with many well known Korean Artists.





Achievements

  • She set a new record, in 2013 on Hidden Singer VS with 98,548 points.

  • In 2019, she won the Gaon Chart Music Awards Artist of the Year with "100 Degree".

  • In 2020, she won the Gaon Chart Music Awards Artist of the Year with "Thank you for Goodbye".
