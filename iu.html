Achievements

  • She started as a trainee for Loen Entertainment in 2007.

  • She released her first album ‘Lost and Found’ on 24 September 2008.

  • She has been honored by the Ministry of Culture, Sports and Tourism for her work as a musician and was recognized as a s 2008.

  • She has been recognized as a style icon by the Style Icon Awards in 2011.

  • Upon its release, Last Fantasy was a commercial success, selling almost seven million copies of digital tracks in its first week of release. The total number of its digital sales surpassed the 10 million mark the following week. The album sold over 117,000 copies, making it the 15th best-selling Korean album in 2011.

  • IU was appointed as the honorary ambassador for EXPO 2012 Yeosu Korea.

  • In 2012, IU was identified as one of the most influential people in South Korea by the Forbes Korea Power Celebrity list, and has since been mentioned five times.

  • IU won Song of the Year at both the 3rd Melon Music Awards and the 9th Korean Music Awards for her single "Good Day".

  • Won Record of the Year (Digital) at the 20th Seoul Music Awards and Best Solo Vocal Performance at the 13th Mnet Asian Music Awards.

  • The single gathered a total of seven trophies on three music programs including Music Bank, Inkigayo and M Countdown

  • She won Record of the Year (Album) and Best Female Artist at the 14th Mnet Asian Music Awards. She additionally earned ten trophies on music programs for her lead single "You & I".

  • IU has also won several awards for her work as an actress, earning the title of "Best New Actress" at the 2014 KBS Drama Awards for her performance in You're the Best.

  • She also won awards for Best Couple Award with Lee Joon-gi, and Idol Academy Award, Heart-wrenching Award for her drama series Moon Lovers: Scarlet Heart Ryeo.

  • In 2017, IU was one of the most nominated and awarded acts at the 9th Melon Music Awards, winning three out of six nominations, including Album of the Year, Top 10 Artists, and the Best Songwriter Award.

  • SAt the 7th Gaon Chart Music Awards, she was nominated in seven categories winning five, including Lyricist of the Year and Producer of the Year for her third Korean album Palette.

  • Won Best Pop Album for Palette respectively at the 15th Korean Music Awards.

  • At the 2018 Golden Disc Awards, she won two categories including Song of the Year for "Through the Night"

  • She participates in a reality show called ‘Hyori’s Bed & Breakfast’ alongside Lee Hyori and Lee Hyori’s husband, Lee Sang Soon.
