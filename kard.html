K.A.R.D

Fandom Name: Hidden Kard

K.A.R.D (카드) (also stylized as KARD) is a Korean a co-ed group consisting of 4 members: J.Seph, BM, Somin and Jiwoo. They are under DSP Media. Before their official debut they released three project singles Oh NaNa, Don’t Recall and Rumor. They officially debuted on July 19, 2017 with the EP Hola Hola.
